About COVID

Symptoms of COVID 19

Most common symptoms: There are many symptoms of COVID-19, the common symptoms include; fever, dry cough, tiredness, sore throat, loss of taste and smell. More serious symptoms include, difficulty breathing, or shortness of breath, chest pain, and loss of speech or movement. .

Who Is At Risk?

Many people are at risk of being infected by the coronavirus. This includes people who work in jobs that require them to have direct contact with other people. Some people are more vulnerable to become infected by the coronavirus, this could be people who are; over the age of 60, having one less kidney, or damage to other organs of the body or having diabetes.

What is coronavirus?

Coronaviruses are large family of viruses that can cause a range of illnesses. These include the common cold as well as more serious diseases like SARS and the new COVID-19
