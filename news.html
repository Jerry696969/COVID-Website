Who Is At Risk?
Evidence to date suggests that two groups of people are at a higher risk of getting severe COVID-19 disease. These are people over 60 and people with cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer

What is coronavirus?
Coronaviruses are large family of viruses that can cause a range of illnesses. These include the common cold as well as more serious diseases like SARS and the new COVID-19
